Phocam

Expression through photography and videography. Originally started as a photojournalism project for aerial photography in 2017, Phocam (previously Photaero) has developed into a brand that focuses on visual arts and content creation. Cinematography becomes the theme of video projects that incorporate in-house music pieces. Various photography styles like portrait, night, and architecture are areas of growing expertise. As these works satisfy our creative drive, the primary goal of Phocam is to share interesting ideas and collaborate on creative projects. It is a brand that ultimately seeks to provide value to collaborators, and perspective through visual art.
